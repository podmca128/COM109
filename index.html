About Us

At Elevate Gym, we are proud to be a modern fitness centre dedicated to helping individuals of all levels achieve their health and wellness goals. Located at 125–127 Main Street, Clogher, BT76 0AA, United Kingdom, our facility is equipped with a wide range of high-quality equipment to support everything from strength training to functional workouts. Whether you're building strength, improving endurance or just getting started. Elevate Gym provides a welcoming and energising environment where you can train with confidence and purpose.
